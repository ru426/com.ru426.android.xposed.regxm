Module of the Xposed framework.
Tested with (SO04-D) JB4.1 Xperia GX in Japan.
Can add functionality with the addition of its own plugin.
Improvement of freedom and stability By Filter function.

■SystemUI features
- Add Brightness bar to NotificationBar.
- Change Brightness bar position to bottom.
- Change the tools toggle position to bottom.
- Carrier name show/hide on NotificationBar.


■SystemUI GXMod features(Only SO-04D with 154Mod in Japan)
- Disable RecentList on Long press HOME key.
- Enable OptionalKey Long press function.

and many more...
